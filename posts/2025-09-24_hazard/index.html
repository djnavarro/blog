Hazard and survival functions

Suppose we have a probability density function \(f(t)\) with cumulative density function \(F(t)\), the hazard function is

\[ h(t) = \frac{f(t)}{1-F(t)} \]

Survival function

\[ S(t) = 1 - F(t) \]
